## Product claims and evidence basis

- CBD and CBG are used at **clinically relevant concentrations**, not trace amounts.
- Both molecules interact with the endocannabinoid system (ECS), specifically CB1/CB2 receptors present in human skin, and with TRPV receptors.
- Organic jojoba oil is non-comedogenic (comedogenic rating 0–2) and structurally similar to human sebum (wax esters), making it barrier-compatible.
- No fragrances, no essential oils, no synthetic preservatives. pH of oil base is neutral to skin.
- All products are manufactured in Sweden under GMP.

## Routine logic

- **Morning**: Cleanse with warm water only → apply The ONE Facial Oil (2–3 drops) → optional: TA-DA Serum booster → sunscreen last.
- **Evening**: Remove makeup with Au Naturel → apply I LOVE Facial Oil (2–3 drops) → optional: TA-DA Serum booster.
- **Frequency of TA-DA Serum**: Daily if desired, or as a weekly booster.
- **Supplement (Fungtastic)**: 2 capsules per day, ideally with food.

## What 1753 does NOT sell or recommend

- No fragranced creams.
- No foaming cleansers with SLS/SLES.
- No actives that strip the barrier (high-percent AHAs, retinol for beginners).
- No products that require layering more than 3 steps.
