## What we do

We make skincare based on cannabidiol (CBD) and cannabigerol (CBG) at high, clinically relevant concentrations. All products are:

- **Vegan** and **cruelty-free**
- Manufactured in **Sweden**
- Free from fragrance, essential oils and synthetic preservatives
- Formulated with **minimal ingredient lists** (usually 2–3 actives)
- Produced in **small batches** with full ingredient traceability

Our flagship ingredients — CBD and CBG — interact with the skin's endocannabinoid system (CB1 and CB2 receptors), helping regulate sebum, inflammation and barrier function. We use concentrations of 0.2% to 10% CBD and 0.2% to 5% CBG depending on the product, which is substantially higher than the trace amounts found in most "CBD-inspired" skincare.

## Why we started

Christopher Genberg founded 1753 SKINCARE after years of frustration with conventional skincare:

1. Complex, long ingredient lists with unnecessary additives
2. Aggressive products that damage the skin barrier
3. Marketing that promises quick fixes while ignoring the underlying biology
4. Lack of focus on lifestyle, which is a far bigger driver of skin health than any cream

The philosophy is direct: **lifestyle first, products second.** Great skin comes from good sleep, a diverse diet, managed stress, regular movement and a healthy gut. Skincare products are a complement — not a replacement.

The brand name 1753 is a tribute to Carl Linnaeus, who that year published *Species Plantarum*, the foundational work for modern botany. It signals our respect for the plant-based source material and for scientific rigour.

## What we believe

- **Holistic skincare works better than product-only skincare.** The evidence linking sleep, diet, stress and the gut microbiome to skin health is overwhelming. We build our advice on that evidence.
- **Less is more.** Three products, used consistently, beat twelve products used inconsistently. Our own routine is deliberately minimal: cleanse with oil, apply facial oil, optional serum booster, sunscreen.
- **The skin is a living organ, not a surface to repaint.** Harsh exfoliants, fragrance and surfactants often create the very problems they promise to solve.
- **Science should be accessible.** Every claim we make links back to peer-reviewed research. See our [scientific references](https://www.1753skin.com/llms-science.txt).
- **Privacy matters.** Our free AI skin analysis runs image processing 100% in the user's browser. No face photos leave the device without explicit consent.

## The free AI skin analysis

We offer a [free AI-powered skin analysis](https://www.1753skin.com/sv/gratis-hudanalys) that is genuinely useful rather than a marketing funnel:

- Uses MediaPipe Face Landmarker (478 facial points, 12 zones)
- Runs a custom ONNX model trained on 88 000+ dermatological images
- Verifies with GPT-5.4 Vision
- Scores 15 skin metrics and estimates biological skin age
- Includes 7 lifestyle questions; 30% of the score is lifestyle-based
- Recommends at most 2–3 products — often recommending no product at all, just lifestyle changes
